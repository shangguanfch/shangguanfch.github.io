Posted on 2017-08-02 Edited on 2022-10-21 In record Symbols count in article: 8.7k Reading time ≈ 8 mins.

Across the Great Wall we can reach every corner in the world.

The Great Firewall of China (GFW) is the combination of legislative and technological actions that have been taken by the government of Mainland China. For more information, visit https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Firewall.
